Everything you need to know about KissMoKa Foundation products and services.

Which shade is best for me?

+
First, determine your undertone (cool, neutral, or warm) using the vein test or jewelry test. Then choose your depth: Light (01-03), Medium (04-06), or Deep (07-08). For fair skin with cool undertones, try Porcelain or Ivory. For medium warm skin, Natural or Honey work beautifully. For deep warm skin, Caramel or Mocha are perfect. Still unsure? Message us on LINE with a selfie in natural lighting, and we'll recommend your perfect match!

Is KissMoKa foundation safe for sensitive or acne-prone skin?

+
Yes! KissMoKa foundation is dermatologically tested, non-comedogenic (won't clog pores), and formulated without parabens, sulfates, or fragrances. It's safe for sensitive and acne-prone skin. The formula includes skin-loving ingredients like hyaluronic acid and vitamin E that actually benefit your skin. However, if you have specific allergies, contact us for a full ingredient list.

How long does the foundation last on the skin?

+
KissMoKa foundation is formulated for 12-hour wear. It stays fresh throughout your day without oxidizing, fading, or caking. For oily skin, we recommend setting the T-zone with powder for maximum longevity. The foundation is also transfer-resistant once set, so it won't rub off on your phone or clothes.

What is the coverage level?

+
KissMoKa foundation offers buildable coverage from sheer to medium. One layer gives you a natural, skin-like finish that evens out your complexion. Two layers provide medium coverage that conceals blemishes and discoloration while still looking natural. You can customize the coverage based on your needs and preferences.

What is the finish—matte, dewy, or natural?

+
KissMoKa foundation has a natural, skin-like finish—not too matte, not too dewy. It gives a healthy, radiant glow that looks like real skin. For oily skin, you can set with powder for a more matte finish. For dry skin, skip powder or add a drop of facial oil for extra dewiness. The versatile formula adapts to your preference!

Does it work well in hot, humid weather?

+
Yes! KissMoKa foundation is specifically formulated for Asian climates. It's humidity-resistant, transfer-resistant, and stays fresh in hot weather. For extra staying power in humid conditions, use an oil-free moisturizer, set your T-zone with powder, and finish with setting spray. The lightweight formula won't feel heavy or slide off in the heat.

Is it cruelty-free and vegan?

+
KissMoKa is 100% cruelty-free! We never test on animals at any stage of product development. While our foundation formula is not fully vegan (it contains some natural derivatives), we're committed to ethical beauty practices. Contact us for specific ingredient information if you have concerns.

How much product is in each bottle?

+
Each bottle contains 30ml of foundation with a pump dispenser for easy, hygienic application. With daily use, one bottle typically lasts 2-3 months. The pump ensures you dispense the perfect amount each time without waste.

Does it have SPF? Do I still need sunscreen?

+
Yes! KissMoKa foundation has SPF 30 broad-spectrum protection. For daily activities, this is sufficient sun protection. However, if you'll be outdoors for extended periods, we recommend layering additional sunscreen underneath. The SPF in our foundation is an added bonus, not a replacement for dedicated sun protection during intense sun exposure.

How should I store my foundation?

+
Store your foundation in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid leaving it in your car or bathroom (humidity can affect the formula). Keep the cap tightly closed when not in use. Proper storage helps maintain the quality and extends the shelf life. Use within 12 months of opening for best results.

What if I have an allergic reaction?

+

If you experience any irritation, redness, or discomfort, discontinue use immediately and wash off the product. Consult a healthcare professional if symptoms persist.
