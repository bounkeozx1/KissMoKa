Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KissMoKa products and services.

How do I order KissMoKa lipsticks?

+
You can order through our social media channels: LINE Official, Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok. Simply click on any product's "Order Now" button, and it will direct you to our LINE Official account where our team will assist you with your order. You can also message us directly on any of our social platforms with the product name and your delivery details.

What is the price of each lipstick?

+
All KissMoKa lipsticks are priced at ฿299 each. This affordable price point makes it easy to try multiple shades and build your collection without breaking the bank. We believe premium quality should be accessible to everyone, especially students and young professionals.

Which shade is best for me?

+
The best shade depends on your skin tone and personal preference. For fair skin, try Soft Rose or Petal Pink. Medium skin tones look beautiful with Nude Blush or Coral Dream. If you have warm undertones, consider Honey Nude or Coral Dream. For cool undertones, Berry Whisper or Mauve Mist are excellent choices. You can also message us with your skin tone description, and we'll recommend the perfect shade for you!

Are KissMoKa lipsticks safe for sensitive skin?

+
Yes! All KissMoKa lipsticks are dermatologically tested and formulated with gentle ingredients suitable for sensitive skin. We use moisturizing components like natural oils and vitamin E, and avoid harsh chemicals that can irritate delicate lips. However, if you have specific allergies, please contact us for detailed ingredient information.

How long does the lipstick last?

+
KissMoKa lipsticks are designed for long-lasting wear, typically lasting 4-6 hours depending on your activities. You may need to reapply after eating or drinking. The moisturizing formula ensures your lips stay comfortable throughout the day without drying out.

Do you offer shipping? How much does it cost?

+
Yes, we offer shipping throughout Thailand. Shipping costs vary based on your location and order size. For Bangkok and surrounding areas, shipping is typically ฿40-60. For other provinces, it ranges from ฿60-100. Orders over ฿500 may qualify for free shipping. Please contact us through LINE or Facebook for exact shipping rates to your area.

How long does delivery take?

+
For Bangkok and surrounding areas, delivery typically takes 1-3 business days. For other provinces, it may take 3-5 business days. We ship orders within 24 hours of confirmation and payment. You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept various payment methods for your convenience: bank transfer, PromptPay, mobile banking, and cash on delivery (for select areas). Payment details will be provided when you place your order through our social media channels.

Can I return or exchange a product?

+
We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery if the product is unopened and in original condition. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened lipsticks unless there is a manufacturing defect. If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately with photos, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.

Are your lipsticks vegan or cruelty-free?

+
Yes! KissMoKa is proud to be cruelty-free. We do not test on animals at any stage of product development. While not all our products are vegan (some contain beeswax or other natural derivatives), we're committed to ethical beauty practices. Please contact us if you need specific ingredient information for any product.

How should I store my KissMoKa lipstick?

+
Store your lipsticks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid leaving them in your car or in direct sunlight, as heat can melt the formula. Keep the cap tightly closed when not in use. Proper storage will help maintain the quality and extend the life of your lipstick.

Can I buy KissMoKa products in physical stores?

+
Currently, KissMoKa is available exclusively online through our social media channels. This allows us to keep prices affordable while maintaining quality. We're exploring retail partnerships for the future, so stay connected with us on social media for updates!

Do you have a rewards or loyalty program?

+
Yes! We regularly run promotions and special offers for our loyal customers. Follow us on LINE, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok to stay updated on flash sales, bundle deals, and exclusive discounts. We also offer special promotions for students—just ask our team when you order!

What if I have an allergic reaction?

+
While our products are tested and safe for most users, individual reactions can vary. If you experience any irritation, discontinue use immediately and consult a healthcare professional. Please contact us right away so we can assist you and provide a full refund if necessary. Your safety is our top priority.

How can I contact customer service?

+
You can reach us through any of our social media platforms: LINE Official (fastest response), Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok. Our customer service team is available Monday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Thailand time). We typically respond within a few hours during business hours.
