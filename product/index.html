How to Find Your Perfect Shade

Follow our simple guide to match your skin tone

1

Check Your Undertone

Cool: Veins look blue/purple → Try Porcelain, Ivory

Neutral: Veins look blue-green → Try Ivory, Natural

Warm: Veins look green → Try Beige, Honey, Tan, Caramel, Mocha

2

Determine Your Depth

Light: Shades 01-03 (Porcelain to Beige)

Medium: Shades 04-06 (Natural to Tan)

Deep: Shades 07-08 (Caramel to Mocha)

3

Test on Your Jawline

The perfect shade disappears into your skin on your jawline. Not too light, not too dark—just right!
